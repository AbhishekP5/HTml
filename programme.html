Kampus To Korporate

Kampus to Korporate programme provides new GTEs with training and development analysis and also insight on the Korporate strategy. Not only soft skills to survive in the Korporate but also technical training will be given on the Kampus to Korporate training.

Induction

welcoming newly recruited employees and supporting them to adjust to their new roles and working environments.

Training

The training period includes knowing how the Company works providing the new GTEs with the knowledge and skills they need to perform their jobs at a high level.

Assesment

Assesment of the training is done by leaders and experts of their respective feilds.
